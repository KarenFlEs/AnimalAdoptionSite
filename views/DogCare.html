Dog Care

Here are the following instructions on how to properly take care of a dog

General Tips

  • Regularly groom your dog
  • Schedule annual check-ups with your vet
  • Maintain proper vaccinations
  • Consider spaying or neutering your pet
  • Brush your dog's teeth
  • Use a leash while in public

Feeding

  • Puppies eight to 12 weeks old need 4 meals per day
  • Feed puppies 3 to 6 months old three meals a day
  • Feed puppies 6 months to 1 year two meals a day
  • After 1 year old, usually have one meal a day
  • For larger canines, it is better for some to feed two smaller meals
  • Provide sufficient water throughout the day
You can see from this site for the foods that a dog can't eat

Exercise

Dogs need to exercise to stimulate their minds and stay healthy. It also helps to avoid boredum, and overall for the dog to live a happy life. The individual exercises vary on the breed, age and level of health.
