Cat Care

Here are the following instructions on how to properly take care of a cat

General Tips

  • Provide it with a stimulating and safe environment
  • Grrom it regurlaly especially with daily grooming
  • Vaccinate against the major feline diseases regularly
  • Provide the cat with outdoor access or be prepared to empty and clean the litter tray daily
  • Worm regularly and provide treatment for fleas

Feeding

  • Make sure to give your cat a balanced meal
  • Cats require taurine for their heart and eye health
  • Threats should be no more than 5-10% of the diet
  • Provide fresh, clean water at all times
  • If your cat is refusing food, please check with your vetenerian
